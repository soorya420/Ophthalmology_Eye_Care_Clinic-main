Transit & Parking Guide

Patient Parking

Free parking is validated for up to 3 hours in the Green Medical Garage located directly behind the clinic. Please bring your ticket to the front reception desk for scanning.

Valet parking: Available from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM at the main entrance.

Public Transit

Our clinic is located 2 blocks east of the Metro Plaza Subway Station (Lines Central and Northern). Bus routes 14, 27, and 104 stop directly in front of our clinic building.

Accessibility: Low-floor shuttle drop-off access at the East Portico.

Surgical Patients

For LASIK or cataract surgery, you must arrange an escort to accompany you home post-op. Rideshare services are permitted only if the driver has an escort helper present.

Note: Our discharge nurse coordinates pick-up at the covered parking lobby.
