Insurance & Financing Options

Accepted Insurance

We accept major PPO insurance networks and Medicare plans. Our administrative staff coordinates claims directly for diagnostic exams and surgical consultations:

  • Blue Cross Blue Shield
  • Aetna PPO
  • Cigna Health
  • UnitedHealthcare
  • Medicare Part B

Note: Out-of-network benefits may apply. Please contact our billing coordinator at checkout to verify your copay.

Financing & Health Accounts

We support tax-free healthcare accounts and low-interest payment plans for elective laser vision correction and premium lens implants:

  • HSA / FSA Compatible: Pay for custom wavefront LASIK, LipiFlow, or prescription frames using pre-tax funds.
  • CareCredit financing: 0% interest payment plans for up to 24 months on surgical visual corrections.
