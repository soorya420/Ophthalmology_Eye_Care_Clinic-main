Our Story
Since 2012

Decades of Eye Clinical Innovation

Founded by Dr. Michael Vance, the clinic began as a small vision research unit. Over the years, we integrated wave-front robotics and digital diagnostic dashboards, leading ophthalmology practices in the region.

Today, our board-certified surgeons collaborate with global healthcare foundations to refine non-invasive laser techniques, reducing corneal flap recovery times to under 24 hours.

Inside Our Clinic

Our World-Class Facilities

WaveLight Surgical Suite

Our clean-room surgery center features the elite WaveLight Femtosecond laser suite. It maintains active HEPA air filtration and stable temperature metrics to ensure total safety and micron precision calibration.

Advanced Diagnostics Wing

Equipped with 3D corneal topographers, high-res optical coherence tomography (OCT) scanners, and digital refractors. Allows quick, drop-free mapping of cell structures and fluid flow metrics.

Designer Optical Boutique

Our professional boutique showcases handpicked European designer frames, custom progressive lenses, and computer blue-light filters, with specialist opticians providing on-site fittings.
